What services do you offer?

We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance for standard events, and one month for special occasions or travel arrangements. However, we do our best to accommodate last-minute requests.

We understand that plans can change. Cancellations made 48 hours or more in advance receive a full refund. Last-minute cancellations are handled on a case-by-case basis.
